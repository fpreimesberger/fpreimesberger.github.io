    GeneMoji

    Node.js app that renders an avatar based on the user's DNA

    Users who've been sequenced through 23andMe can connect their account to GeneMoji, which pulls relevant genetic data. Data is run through an algorithm that detects common genetic variants and predicts the most likely physical characteristics. These traits are then used to build an avatar meant to resemble the user.




    Big Mood

    Node.js app that reads user's mood and returns a matching Spotify playlist

    Users can upload a photo of their face and the app will read their mood using Microsoft Azure's facial recognition technology. A random Spotify playlist that matches how they're feeling is returned.
